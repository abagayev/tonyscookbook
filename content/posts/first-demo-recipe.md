## Time

- Prep: 5 minutes
- Cook: 12 minutes
- Total: 17 minutes

## Ingredients (2 servings)

- 200 g spaghetti
- 3 tbsp butter
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 1/4 tsp chili flakes (optional)
- 2 tbsp reserved pasta water
- 2 tbsp grated parmesan
- Salt and black pepper, to taste
- Chopped parsley, for serving

## Steps

1. Boil salted water and cook spaghetti until al dente.
2. Reserve a little pasta water, then drain the pasta.
3. Melt butter in a pan over medium heat and add garlic.
4. Cook garlic for 30–45 seconds until fragrant (do not brown).
5. Add chili flakes, pasta, and a splash of reserved water.
6. Toss until glossy, then season with salt and pepper.
7. Turn off heat, mix in parmesan, and top with parsley.

## Notes

- Add lemon zest for brightness.
- Add cooked shrimp or mushrooms for extra protein.
- Use less butter and more pasta water for a lighter version.
