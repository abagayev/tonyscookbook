I created this cookbook to share practical home recipes that are easy to follow and genuinely tasty. My goal is to keep every post simple, clear, and repeatable.

## What you’ll find here

- Weeknight-friendly meals
- Tested recipes with straightforward steps
- Notes and small tweaks that improve results

## My cooking style

I like balanced flavors, simple ingredients, and techniques that don’t require fancy equipment. If a recipe can be made easier without losing flavor, I’ll usually choose the simpler path.

Thanks for visiting — I hope this site helps you cook more confidently at home.
