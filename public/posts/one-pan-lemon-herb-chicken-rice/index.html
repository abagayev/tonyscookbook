One-Pan Lemon Herb Chicken & Rice

A bright, cozy one-pan dinner with juicy chicken and herby rice.
March 13, 2026 · Tony | Translations:
  • Uk

A simple one-pan meal with fresh lemon flavor, tender chicken, and fluffy rice.

Great for busy evenings: minimal prep, one skillet, easy cleanup.

Time#

  • Prep: 10 minutes
  • Cook: 30 minutes
  • Total: 40 minutes

Ingredients (3 servings)#

  • 500 g boneless chicken thighs
  • 1 cup long-grain rice, rinsed
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 2 tbsp chopped parsley

Steps#

  1. Season chicken with salt, pepper, paprika, and oregano.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet and sear chicken for 3–4 minutes per side.
  3. Remove chicken to a plate.
  4. In the same pan, cook onion for 2 minutes, then add garlic for 30 seconds.
  5. Stir in rice and toast for 1 minute.
  6. Add broth, lemon zest, and lemon juice. Mix well.
  7. Return chicken to the pan, cover, and simmer on low heat for 18–20 minutes.
  8. Turn off heat and rest covered for 5 minutes.
  9. Fluff rice, sprinkle parsley, and serve.

Serving ideas#

  • Add a cucumber yogurt salad on the side.
  • Serve with roasted broccoli or green beans.
  • Finish with extra lemon wedges.

Quick notes#

  • If rice feels dry, add 2–3 tbsp warm broth and rest 2 more minutes.
  • For stronger herb flavor, add a pinch of thyme.
  • For spice, add a small pinch of chili flakes.
