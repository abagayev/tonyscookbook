One-Pan Lemon Herb Chicken & Rice

A simple one-pan meal with fresh lemon flavor, tender chicken, and fluffy rice. Great for busy evenings: minimal prep, one skillet, easy cleanup. Time Prep: 10 minutes Cook: 30 minutes Total: 40 minutes Ingredients (3 servings) 500 g boneless chicken thighs 1 cup long-grain rice, rinsed 2 tbsp olive oil 1 small onion, finely chopped 2 garlic cloves, minced 2 cups chicken broth Zest of 1 lemon 2 tbsp lemon juice 1 tsp dried oregano 1/2 tsp paprika Salt and black pepper, to taste 2 tbsp chopped parsley Steps Season chicken with salt, pepper, paprika, and oregano. Heat oil in a large skillet and sear chicken for 3–4 minutes per side. Remove chicken to a plate. In the same pan, cook onion for 2 minutes, then add garlic for 30 seconds. Stir in rice and toast for 1 minute. Add broth, lemon zest, and lemon juice. Mix well. Return chicken to the pan, cover, and simmer on low heat for 18–20 minutes. Turn off heat and rest covered for 5 minutes. Fluff rice, sprinkle parsley, and serve. Serving ideas Add a cucumber yogurt salad on the side. Serve with roasted broccoli or green beans. Finish with extra lemon wedges. Quick notes If rice feels dry, add 2–3 tbsp warm broth and rest 2 more minutes. For stronger herb flavor, add a pinch of thyme. For spice, add a small pinch of chili flakes.

Demo Recipe: Garlic Butter Pasta

This is the first demo recipe post for the blog. Time Prep: 5 minutes Cook: 12 minutes Total: 17 minutes Ingredients (2 servings) 200 g spaghetti 3 tbsp butter 3 garlic cloves, minced 1/4 tsp chili flakes (optional) 2 tbsp reserved pasta water 2 tbsp grated parmesan Salt and black pepper, to taste Chopped parsley, for serving Steps Boil salted water and cook spaghetti until al dente. Reserve a little pasta water, then drain the pasta. Melt butter in a pan over medium heat and add garlic. Cook garlic for 30–45 seconds until fragrant (do not brown). Add chili flakes, pasta, and a splash of reserved water. Toss until glossy, then season with salt and pepper. Turn off heat, mix in parmesan, and top with parsley. Notes Add lemon zest for brightness. Add cooked shrimp or mushrooms for extra protein. Use less butter and more pasta water for a lighter version.
